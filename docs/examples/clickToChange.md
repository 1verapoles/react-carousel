## Click to change
By default, clicking the slides does nothing. You can change that behavior with clickToChange prop. 
```jsx render
<div>
  <Carousel
    clickToChange
    slidesPerPage={2}
    centered
  >
   <img src={imageOne} />
   <img src={imageTwo} />
   <img src={imageThree} />
  </Carousel>
</div>
```
